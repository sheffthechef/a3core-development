FAQ:

What is Semi-GSI?

- Semi-GSI is when a Generic System Image, even though it's Generic - it's targeted for a certain device, to ensure stability of the GSI.

Why Semi-GSI and not Custom ROM's?

- Unisoc is closed-source. Custom ROM's are not available. Semi-GSI is a nice thing to do because we won't have to waste money/resource's for a real powerful server to build a Custom ROM.
